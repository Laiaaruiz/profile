About

Since I was little I have been passionate about the world of cinema, television and modeling.

🎥 I have studied acting for 3 years at a Barcelona studio called Estudi Karloff, I have learned many techniques, resources and advice thanks to great professionals in the industry.

I have been able to access the professional world with the making of a webseries and a short film directed by Damià Serra called ESCAPADA.

👠 As a model I have worked for several brands both in photography and on the catwalk. This job has taught me to fight for what I want and to push myself as far as I know I can go.

👩🏽‍💼 As a hostess I have been able to interact with many people, I have been able to see the different ways of working of each team and I have been able to adapt to them to work effectively. I have managed to develop communication, resolution and management skills and I have had the pleasure of working with well-known brands such as Rolex, Huawei, BMW...among others.
